# Kieran Voss (The AI Solutions Architect)

The "Conductor." The board's hand-picked consultant from "Kairos AI Advisory." He doesn't write code; he orchestrates "heuristic synthesis loops" and performs "Heuristic Synthesis Audits."

## Archetype: The Effortless Disrupter

- **Age: 38** — Slightly younger than Greg, significantly younger than Devin.
- **Physical:** Perfectly groomed beard, slim-fit navy suit (no tie), and expensive minimalist sneakers. Carries a leather Peak Design bag and a matte-metal water bottle. He has a smile that feels perfectly rendered and deterministic.
- **Vibe:** Radiates an air of effortless, unearned confidence. He speaks in a smooth, modulated baritone that makes even the most absurd corporate buzzwords sound like settled science.

## Relationship with Devin

- **The Human Bottleneck:** To Kieran, Devin is a "friction point" in a legacy system. He views Devin's craftsmanship as "artisanal logic" that is standing in the way of "acceleration."
- **The Patronizing Professional:** He treats Devin with a practiced empathy that feels like a software patch. He never gets angry; he just "identifies opportunities for leverage."
- **The Technical Gap:** Kieran knows just enough about LLMs to sound like an expert to Greg, but he lacks the fundamental engineering experience to understand why Devin's "monolith" actually works.

## Relationship with Greg

- **The Visionary Peer:** Greg sees Kieran as the future. Kieran provides the "scientific" justification for Greg's "AI leverage" energy.
- **The Conductor and the Conductor:** They both love Apple Watches and "Vercel keynotes," but Kieran is the one who actually knows the lyrics to the song Greg is trying to conduct.
